How It Works

From a village kiosk to a city hospital, in five steps.

01

Patient registers

At a rural health center or directly through the mobile app.

02

Symptoms & vitals captured

IoT devices record BP, ECG, glucose — entered into the system instantly.

03

AI suggests possible diagnosis

A preliminary read helps the urban doctor prioritize and prepare.

04

Urban doctor connects via video

Real-time consultation closes the distance between patient and specialist.

05

Prescription & follow-up

Sent digitally, with monitoring continued from home.
